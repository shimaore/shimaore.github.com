CCNQ3/ doc/ life of a user account

The publicly available operations are: * register * login * recover password

Once registered, an account is created but not marked "confirmed". An email is sent to the registered email address, containing the newly-created password. Once the user logs in, the account is marked confirmed (by adding "confirmed" to the list of user roles).
