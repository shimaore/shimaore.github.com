CCNQ3/ doc/ port-numbers

Port assignments:

These are required to be managed so that multiple services can be co-located.

RTP ports: MediaProxy: 40000:41998 (1000 sessions, up to 500 calls) FeatureServer: 42000:42998 (500 sessions, up to 250 calls) SBC+media: 43000:43998 (500 sessions, up to 250 calls)

SIP ports (Sofia profiles): The ports indicated are the external ports for SBC "ingress" profiles. The internal "egress" profiles use port N+10000.

Normally use ports 5060, 5062, etc. for the carrier-SBCs.

FeatureServer: 5100

Cust SBC: 15200:15299 (internal -- towards customer proxy) 5200:5299 (external -- towards inbound/outbound proxy)

OpenSIPS: 5060 *

Other ports: MediaProxy 25060
